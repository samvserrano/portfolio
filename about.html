    • Born and raised in Phoenix, Arizona, Samuel (he/him/his) likes to take inspiration from all things that have a unique design and presence. As a Latino American, Samuel takes advantage of his inspiration from his culture and ethnicity and as a member of the LGBTQIA+ community, Samuel also finds ways to make his art about inclusivity and bringing people together through design. As he gets older, he looks for new inspirations and opportunities for staying within the culture and keeping his knowledge fresh about art and design.


    Samuel V Serrano (Sam V Serrano) is a designer that specializes in all forms of designs. Samuel began his journey at 22, attending Glendale Community College in Glendale Arizona, focusing on graphic design. Ever since he was a child, he participated in the arts whether it was music, painting, or story writing. Samuel currently attends Arizona State University, at the Ira A. Fulton Schools of Engineering, and majoring in the Graphic Information Technology. Samuel has a focus on graphic design and web design. However, he also takes part in graphic drawing and 3D modeling on his free time. Since at ASU, Samuel has taken many accelerated courses on website design and development. He's learned the basics of HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Samuel currently lives in Phoenix, Arizona with his pet cat, Raven. When Samuel is not working, you can find him reading about art history, digital drawing through Procreate on his iPad, or watercolor painting in a sketchbook. Samuel does enjoy playing video games, listening to music, and learning about photography.
