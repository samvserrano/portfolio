Catalyst Bumper & Miami Dolphins Lower Thirds


PBS Catalyst

Catalyst is a video broadcast exploring scientific research at Arizona State University. The main focus of the project was to illustrate and create a broadcast bumper for the platform. The bumper would be used as the intro or outro of their web videos. The process included a creative brief and storyboard to ilustrate a visual presentation of how the bumper would be created.

Miami Dolphins Lower Thirds


Miami Dolphins are a US football team part of the NFL. The main focus on this project was to show a animated window of descriptions that would play during a game. The lower thirds could be used to express a score, a penalty, or info about a player. These animations appear at the bottom of a platforms screen for viewers.
