    Global Pristine is a fictional brand, that produces skincare. The project concept was to declare how a brand name can be used within different design themes. With this project, I created 5 different variations of Global Pristine that correlate with how skincare is sold in department stores based on pricing and notability. You have traditional, low budget, organic, modern, and expensive. These designs have been based on real world brands that exist today such as Nuetrogena, Cerave, Burts Bees, The Ordinary, and La Mer.
