Global Pristine is a fictional brand, that produces skincare. The project concept was to declare how a brand name can be used within different design themes. With this project, I created 5 different variations of Global Pristine that correlate with how skincare is sold in establishments. You have traditional, low budget, organic, modern, and expensive.
