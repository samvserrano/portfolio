CHROMATICA was a shift in pop music of 2020. Lady Gaga's 6th studio album encapsulated a cyberpunk-ish themed and created realistic imagery of the future and combining apolcalyptic themes with current events today. For this project, the challenge was mixing futuristic ideas with vintage tones. Heavily inspired by psychedelic art of the 60s/70s, movie posters of the 80s, and grunge textures of the 90s.

MISS ANTHROPOCENE is the fifth studio album from musician Grimes. The album concept revolves around current events and climate change. My rendition of the cover art follows along those lines by presenting a backdrop image of a dried up lake, watery text, and a small graphic near the title of an asteroid hitting Earth. The art combines minimalism, using negative space to portray the emptiness of the lake and our lack of care for the planet.
