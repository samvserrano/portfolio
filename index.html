About Me

Samuel V Serrano (he/him/his) is a seasoned designer based in Phoenix, Arizona, celebrated for his passion for distinctive aesthetics and inclusive designs. Growing up immersed in the vibrant cultural mosaic of the Latino community and navigating the complexities of the LGBTQIA+ spectrum, Samuel draws profound inspiration from his heritage and personal journey. Infusing his designs with unity and inclusivity, he aims to celebrate diversity and foster a sense of belonging through his work.
With a background in graphic design from Glendale Community College and a BS in Graphic Information Technology from Arizona State University, Samuel continually refines his craft. Residing in Phoenix with his feline companion, Raven, he balances his professional dedication with diverse interests, including art history, digital drawing, watercolor painting, and exploration of video games, music, and photography.
