    GHOST IN THE SHELL(1995) is a film based on the manga (titled the same), set in a dystopian Tokyo, in year 2029. The film became a highlight of cyberpunk, futurism,and dystopian imagery. The challenge for this poster was to involve those elements and apply them within a grungy/90s visual, the era of when it was released. The typeface, for the title, was created by myself and created within Adobe Illustrator.
