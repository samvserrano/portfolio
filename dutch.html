DUTCH BROS APP UI/UX

DUTCH BROS

Mobile App UI/UX Case Study

Dutch Bros is a beloved coffee chain known for its laid-back atmosphere and flavorful drinks. Originating in Oregon, Dutch Bros has grown into a West Coast sensation, captivating coffee enthusiasts with its vibrant culture and commitment to quality. From handcrafted espresso beverages to refreshing smoothies, Dutch Bros offers a diverse menu to satisfy every craving.


Although the current app is a great way of captivating the brand, it needed improvement to create a cohesive visual for newer generations of tech and design. With an in-app item purchase, the app creates an upgraded effect for customers to receive their items as soon as possible.

The presented visuals showcase screen captures of how the app would look, being used in this context. Navigating the app menu to find the item menu, locating a specific drink to then have it be added to the app shopping bag. This leads into purchasing the item and being notified of the location of where the shop is being made.

The app could present more features. However, the purpose of this case study was to improve on visuals, user experience, and creating an online shopping experience that has competition with other similar establishments.
